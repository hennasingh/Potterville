Step into the enchanting world of magic, where wands choose their wizards, mythical creatures roam, and extraordinary adventures await. From the cobblestone streets of Diagon Alley to the hallowed halls of Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, the Wizarding World has captivated the hearts and imaginations of millions.

Join us on a journey through this magical universe. Test your knowledge on spells, potions, magical creatures, and the heroic tales of Harry Potter and his friends. Are you ready to prove your prowess and show that you're a true Potterhead? Let the magic begin!

Potterville is a word answer game where you will be asked questions about the Harry Potter world. You will be prompted to choose a category before you start the quiz. There will be 10 questions in the quiz.

  • The Dark Arts
  • Hogwarts
  • Magical Spells and Potions
  • Magical Objects
  • Magical People
  • Animals & Magical Creatures

You will be given a random enemy to fight with. Your enemy starts at 100 power. Each correct answer will earn you a magical item that can help you defeat your enemy. You win the game if you are successful in defeating the enemy.
